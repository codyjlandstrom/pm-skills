# Claude Skills

A collection of custom skills for Claude that extend its capabilities for product management and discovery workflows.

## Skills

### [Mom Test](skills/mom-test/)

Applies Rob Fitzpatrick's "The Mom Test" framework to product discovery work. Helps you write better interview questions, analyze customer conversations for bias and weak signals, and extract the riskiest assumptions from product ideas.

**Modes:**
- **Question Review** - Evaluate draft interview questions for hypotheticals, leading language, and compliment-fishing
- **Question Generation** - Generate discovery questions from a hypothesis without revealing what you want to hear
- **Conversation Analysis** - Analyze call transcripts for deflections, missing specifics, and buried signals
- **Assumption Extraction** - Surface and rank the riskiest assumptions in a feature spec or product idea

**Install:** Download the latest [mom-test.skill](releases/mom-test.skill) and add it to Claude.
